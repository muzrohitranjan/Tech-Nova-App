Our Mission

India's culinary heritage is one of the richest and most diverse in the world. Each region, each state, each community has its own unique recipes that have been passed down through generations. Unfortunately, many of these recipes are at risk of being lost as modern lifestyles change and older generations pass away.


Tech Nova aims to be a digital repository for these precious recipes, creating a platform where anyone can share and discover traditional Indian dishes. We believe that food is not just about nutrition - it's about culture, history, family, and love.


Our platform allows users to submit their family recipes, complete with the cultural stories and traditions that make each dish special. By doing so, we hope to preserve this invaluable part of India's cultural heritage for future generations.
